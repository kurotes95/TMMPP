Tesfaye Mamo - Founder & Visionary

Tesfaye Mamo is the founder and visionary behind Tesfaye Multimedia Productions. As a celebrated Ethiopian author, filmmaker, and media personality, Tesfaye has dedicated his career to shaping the Ethiopian arts and media landscape for over three decades. His leadership and creativity continue to define the company's legacy.

His notable works include landmark video films such as ፍቅር መጨረሻ ("The End of Love") released in 1987 and እተፈንታ ("Fate") from 1997, as well as the acclaimed 28-episode TV drama series ምልስት ("Mleset") on Fana Television. He also hosted the long-running radio show ኪነት መንገድ ("The Way of Art") on Sheger FM 102.1.

Tesfaye’s commitment to cultural preservation, mentorship, and creative excellence continues to guide the company’s mission today.
